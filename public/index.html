About Us

Welcome to Limited , your number one source for all things footwear. We are dedicated to providing you the very best of footwear, with an emphasis on style, creativity, and comfort. When A-Plus-Plus first started out, their passion for eco-friendly-custom shoes drove them to start their own business. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
